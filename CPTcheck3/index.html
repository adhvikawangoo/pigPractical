Pig Practical Simulator

This website acts as a simulator for the pig practical,
a post pig dissection oral quiz that takes place in highschool
biology. There are two game modes, one is the study guide, where
the player has the ability to read notes about each part of the pig and
study for the second game mode: the quiz. In the quiz the player will be given
75 seconds to answer seven questions and each incorrect answer will result in a
10 second penalty. Good Luck!
